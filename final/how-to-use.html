Simple explanations of what each settings does in the mian homepage

Features

  • Select and roll various types of dice (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, d100)
  • Specify the number of dice you want to roll
  • Add modifiers to the roll (modifiers eather add or subtract to your total roll)
  • View roll results instantly by pushing roll dice
